Bus Time Bot (SG)

Bus Time Bot (SG) is an automated service that works within the Telegram messaging platform, delivering bus arrival timings conveniently. Presently, this bot displays the time to the next bus managed by Go-Ahead, NTU, NUS, SBS Transit, SMRT Buses and Tower Transit.

Experience the convenience now!

Features

You can search via

  • sending your location via GPS

  • choosing your location

  • a bus stop number

  • a postal code

  • popular landmarks

  • an address

  • You can even look up the times of first and last bus when leaving the interchange!

    • Origins

    Started in Dec 2015, the idea started a few months back where Angus Ng, Founder, Hostgagements thought of having a more convenient way (read: lesser taps) of checking bus arrival timings than the official one. As Telegram bots were very popular then, it seemed natural to jump on the bandwagon.

    A demo created by Bernard's friend made him reverse his initial impression of a low usage utility. Moving forward, more bus services and features are constantly added to make it more applicable and accessible to more, contributing to the society, in line with Hostgagements's corporate social responsibility arm.
